For Mobile / Phone

Add WeatherView to your home screen as a free app.

Install on your phone

  1. Android: Chrome menu ⋮ → Add to Home Screen
  2. iPhone: Safari Share → Add to Home Screen
  3. WeatherView works offline after install!
